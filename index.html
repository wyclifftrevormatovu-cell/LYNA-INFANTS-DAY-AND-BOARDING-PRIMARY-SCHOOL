About Us

Lyna Infants’ Day and Boarding Primary School is dedicated to providing high-quality education that develops literacy, numeracy, morals, and essential skills for self-sustainability. Our programs ensure every child becomes a holistic learner ready to thrive in life.

Vision: To become the most successful school in terms of literacy, numeracy, morals, and skills; producing holistic learners for self-sustainability.

Mission: Provision of basic and quality education to learners for self-sustainability.
